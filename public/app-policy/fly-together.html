Information Collection and Use

FlyTogether is an app for flight simulation enthusiasts that allows virtual pilots to connect, organize flights together, and share their passion for simulators such as Microsoft Flight Simulator and X-Plane. The app currently supports the IVAO network.

The app does not collect any personally identifiable information directly. No user account, login, or registration is required to use the Service. All flight data displayed in the app is fetched in real time from the IVAO public API and is not stored by me in any way.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Third-Party Data — IVAO

FlyTogether uses the IVAO API (https://ivao.aero) to retrieve live flight simulation data such as active flights, pilots, and ATC positions on the IVAO network. This data is fetched directly from IVAO and is governed by IVAO's own Privacy Policy. I have no control over the data collected or processed by IVAO.
