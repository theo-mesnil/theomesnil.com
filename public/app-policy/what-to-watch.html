Information Collection and Use

WhatToWatch is the simplest and fastest way to discover movies, series, and where to watch them. The app is powered by React Native (Expo) and the TMDb (The Movie Database) API.

The app requires a login via your TMDb account (https://www.themoviedb.org/). Authentication is handled entirely by TMDb. I do not store your TMDb credentials or any personal account information on my own servers. All data fetched through the TMDb API is governed by TMDb's own Privacy Policy.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
